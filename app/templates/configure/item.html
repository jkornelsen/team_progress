Item Setup

Resources and equipment, or concepts such as upgrades. Often produced according to recipes.

Storage type is important to consider. Universal is good for idle progress, while carried is typically for RPGs. Crafting stations reside at a location. To store more than one way, create an additional item and convert with a recipe. For example, funds in the bank (universal) → cash on hand (carried).
